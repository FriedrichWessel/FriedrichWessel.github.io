From Film Effects to Sustainable Development

With a journey that began in film effects and evolved into a Senior Game Developer role at InnoGames GmbH, I bring over a decade of expertise across all facets of game and software development. My career has spanned from co-founding a company to freelancing as a game developer, from web applications to low-level hardware programming, and from 48h jams to multi year gaming productions. Passionate about sustainability, I design software architectures that boost team productivity and create lasting, memorable experiences. As a tech lead, and experienced trainer, I thrive on sharing knowledge through live coding, professional training, and insightful blog posts. Beyond software, my commitment to sustainability drives my volunteer work. Growing increasingly concerned about the climate crisis and planetary boundaries, I've sought to leverage my skills for a more sustainable future. I volunteer at my village's energy initiative, providing essential software tools and project planning expertise. Driven by a love for our environment and a belief in the power of positive stories, I am dedicated to building a sustainable future. Let’s create something amazing together!
